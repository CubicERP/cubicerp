# Cubic ERP 2020

Cubic ERP Community Edition is a suite of open source apps for business and government processes, available on mobile & web devices, based on LGPL license.

CubicERP Peru S.R.L is the company that lead this project, our experience in ERPs started in 2009, having served multinational corporations, large companies and small businesses throughout Latin America, providing training services, consulting and software development. Contact us at http://cubicerp.com.
